About me

Thanks for looking at my artwork and paintings.

Let me tell you a little about me. I grew up in Greystones, Co. Wicklow where special moments were in abundance. With Greystones, Wicklow and Ireland firmly embedded in my DNA those memories moved with me to the UK where I live.

I picked up a paint brush in 2018 when I moved to wheelchair living due to deteriorating MS health and curious to know what this painting malarkey was all about. I signed up to a mixed media class in Cheltenham and quickly found acrylics captured my imagination and my heart! And I have not looked back. With encouragement from friends and demand from people wanting to send a little bit of Greystones overseas (or capture their own memories), I started selling my paintings and artwork in 2019.

My Work

  • My paintings are in four collections –
  • Greystones The Way it was,
  • Greystones Today,
  • Greystones Timeless,
  • and Other Beautiful Places.

I have captured special memories in painting commissions for more than 70 clients, provided greeting cards to estate agents for new home owners moving into Greystones, sold hundreds of greeting cards, mounted prints, calendars, and gift boxes through a variety of local shops, and through social media.
