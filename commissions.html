Commissions

I normally allow 3 – 4 weeks for a commission, I have outlined the process below. However, if you have a particular date in mind, I will do my best to meet your needs.

The price of a commission is from £250/ €290 for a mounted framed 20”x16” (51 x 41cm), or canvas board 18”x24” (45x65cm). This includes delivery and 5 greeting cards of your painting.

Commission process



Step 1

You get in touch even with the vaguest of ideas, or if you have photos you hunt them out, or ask me to research possible ideas. There is a visual example of how I work below.

Step 2

We talk through our combined thoughts and you decide if you want to proceed. If so, we discuss sizes and type of finish etc. Payment is made to confirm your commission.

Step 3

I keep you updated with progress as I work, sending you photos of my process, hopefully giving you confidence in what will result. I send you a photo of the final painting for your approval (please note, I do hold the final decision regarding design rights)

Step 4

You look forward to receiving your commission in the post.

Please allow 3 -4 weeks from the date payment is taken.
